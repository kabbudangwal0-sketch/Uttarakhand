Culture of Uttarakhand

Discover the traditions, festivals, and lifestyle that define Devbhumi.

Rich Heritage & Traditions

Uttarakhand’s culture reflects the simplicity and spiritual essence of its people. The state is home to diverse communities, each contributing to a vibrant blend of art, dance, festivals, and folk traditions.

Folk Dances

Traditional dances like Langvir Nritya, Barada Nati, and Chholiya are performed during festivals and celebrations.

Local Cuisine

Delicious dishes like Kafuli, Bhatt ki Churkani, and Aloo ke Gutke represent the authentic flavors of the mountains.

Festivals

Festivals like Harela, Bhitauli, and Ghee Sankranti celebrate nature, harvest, and harmony.
