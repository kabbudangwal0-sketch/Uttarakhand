Famous Places of Uttarakhand

Explore the sacred temples, breathtaking mountains, and peaceful valleys of Devbhumi.

Top Destinations

From divine Char Dham temples to serene hill stations, Uttarakhand offers an unmatched mix of spirituality and natural beauty.

Kedarnath

One of the holiest Shiva temples and part of the Char Dham circuit, located amid the Garhwal Himalayas.

Badrinath

Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, Badrinath is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites and a symbol of devotion and peace.

Mussoorie

Known as the “Queen of Hills,” Mussoorie captivates travelers with its scenic charm and pleasant climate.

Travel Tips & Did You Know?

Kedarnath

Did you know? Kedarnath opens only from April to November due to heavy snowfall in winter.

Mussoorie

Tip: Best time to visit is March to June to enjoy pleasant weather and clear skies.

Valley of Flowers

Did you know? This UNESCO World Heritage site blooms with over 500 species of flowers in July-August.
