Tourism in Uttarakhand

From thrilling adventures to peaceful spiritual journeys, discover every shade of Devbhumi.

Adventure Tourism

Uttarakhand is a paradise for adventure lovers. Experience trekking, river rafting, skiing, and paragliding in the lap of nature.

River Rafting - Rishikesh

Enjoy thrilling rafting on the Ganga River surrounded by scenic hills and spiritual vibes.

Skiing - Auli

Glide through the snow-capped slopes of Auli, one of India’s best skiing destinations.

Trekking - Kedarkantha

One of the most popular treks for beginners, offering breathtaking sunrise views from the summit.

Spiritual Tourism

Uttarakhand is home to ancient temples and sacred rivers that have drawn pilgrims for centuries.

Kedarnath Temple

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, located amidst the mighty Himalayas.

Badrinath Temple

Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, this sacred site is a part of the Char Dham Yatra.

Haridwar Ganga Aarti

The mesmerizing evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri attracts thousands of devotees daily.

Eco-Tourism

With lush forests and pristine valleys, Uttarakhand promotes sustainable travel and wildlife conservation.

Jim Corbett National Park

India’s oldest national park, famous for Bengal tigers and rich biodiversity.

Valley of Flowers

A UNESCO World Heritage Site filled with colorful Himalayan flora and scenic trails.

Binsar Wildlife Sanctuary

Peaceful hill forest offering panoramic views of Nanda Devi and wildlife spotting trails.
